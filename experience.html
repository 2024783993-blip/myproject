👩‍🍳 Internship in Taco Bell

I did a community college internship at Taco Bell Bandar Baru Bangi for 5 months. Here, I was able to learn a lot because

I was in every department such as cashier, making food, frying and topping raw materials. I am happy because I was able to work with very good staff and managers who understood me.

Here, I learned that we can solve all things ourselves and think about something before doing it. I gained a lot during my internship here because I was able to communicate and work in a professional and good environment.

💻 Internship in UiTM Rembau

I did a diploma internship at the Bahagian Penyelidikan, Jaringan Industri, Masyarakat dan Alumni (BPJIM&A) for 8 weeks. I learned how to organize, enter data digitally.

In fact, I was able to use what I had learned during my industrial training. During my industrial training, I was able to improve my communication skills and software skills.
